# FAQ: How much does Protekon cost?

Three tiers. No hidden fees. No per-employee surcharges. No surprise invoices when regulations change.

**Core — $597/month**
Eight platform-wide compliance templates that every California employer needs: IIPP, WVPP, Heat Illness Prevention, HazCom, Emergency Action Plan, and the other foundational programs. These are built for your business, not generic PDFs. Includes training tracking, documentation management, and compliance calendar.

This tier is for single-location businesses that need the baseline done right.

**Professional — $897/month**
Everything in Core, plus vertical-specific compliance programs tailored to your industry. A construction company has different Cal/OSHA obligations than a restaurant or a healthcare clinic. Professional adds the programs your NAICS code requires — not generic add-ons, but the specific standards your industry gets cited for.

Also includes enforcement intelligence: real-time monitoring of Cal/OSHA citation activity in your industry and region. You see what your competitors are getting cited for before the inspector shows up at your door.

This tier is for businesses that want to stay ahead of enforcement trends, not just react to them.

**Multi-Site — $1,297/month**
Everything in Professional, plus multi-location management. Each site gets its own customized IIPP and WVPP — because a warehouse in Ontario has different hazards than an office in Riverside. Consolidated reporting gives you a single dashboard across all locations. Centralized training management ensures nothing falls through the cracks at Site #3 because Site #1 had an emergency.

This tier is for businesses operating across multiple locations who need one system, not five binders.

**All tiers are fully managed.** We build the programs, maintain them when regulations change, and handle the documentation. You run your business. We handle compliance.

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. The work speaks for itself.
