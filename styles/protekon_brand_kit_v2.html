05 — Brand Voice

How PROTEKON
Speaks.

PROTEKON speaks like a commander who has already seen every violation, every fine, every shutdown. Direct. Informed. Relentless. The voice never apologizes, never hedges, never qualifies what it knows to be true.

01
Commanding
Directives, not suggestions. Active voice. Short sentences. Periods. We don't say "you might consider" — we say "do this."
"Stop waiting for an inspection to find out you're out of compliance."
02
Authoritative
We know the law. We state facts, not opinions. Specific numbers over vague claims. No hedging. No "we believe." We've analyzed 431K+ violations and $1.03B in penalties.
"Construction has a 63.2% serious violation rate — the highest of any major industry. We know because we analyzed all 169,021 of them."
03
Protective
Always on the side of the business. We don't shame — we shield. The tone is a trusted ally who won't let it happen to you.
"Nothing reaches you without going through PROTEKON first."
04
Precise
Name the regulation, the CFR section, the penalty amount, the vertical. Every word chosen for exactness. "Fast" becomes "before the inspector arrives."
"Utilities have a 69.9% serious violation rate. 7 in 10 violations are serious or worse. PROTEKON tracks all 6,318 of them."
Write Like This
  • Specific numbers, regulation codes, and fine amounts
  • Start with strong verbs — Stop. Command. Protect. Enforce.
  • Second person: "your business," "your workers," "your exposure"
  • Name the consequence before the solution
  • One idea per sentence. Short paragraphs. Periods.
  • Include Spanish in bilingual audience campaigns
Never Write Like This
  • "We believe our platform could potentially help your compliance journey"
  • Passive voice: "Violations are detected by our AI system"
  • Vague claims: "Significantly reduce violations" — say 70%
  • GRC jargon the customer doesn't use daily
  • Cheerful clichés: "Safety first!" / "Stay compliant!"
  • Apologies, hedges, or qualifications of any kind
